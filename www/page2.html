How to Order Peonies Online

Ordering peonies online is easy and convenient. With just a few clicks, you can have fresh, high-quality peonies delivered straight to your doorstep.

Steps to Order:

  1. Visit a trusted flower supplier like Fabulous Florals.
  2. Browse their collection of peonies and choose your favorite variety.
  3. Select the quantity and add them to your cart.
  4. Provide your shipping details and select a delivery date.
  5. Complete the purchase and wait for your fresh peonies to arrive!

How Does Shipping Work?

Peonies are shipped carefully to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Here’s what to expect:

  • Temperature-Controlled Packaging: Your peonies are packed in special boxes to keep them fresh.
  • Fast Delivery: Most online floral shops offer next-day or 2-day shipping.
  • Care Instructions: Once your peonies arrive, follow the care instructions included in the package to keep them fresh longer.
